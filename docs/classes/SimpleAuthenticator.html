    addRealm(\SimpleUrl $url, $type, $realm) 

    Browsers privatey wildly on their behaviour in this regard. Mozilla ignores the realm and presents only when challenged, wasting bandwidth. IE just carries on presenting until a new challenge occours. SimpleTest tries to follow the spirit of the original standards committee and treats the base URL as the root of a file tree shaped realm.
